About Secret Santa Generator

The Secret Santa Generator is a tool designed to simplify the process of organizing Secret Santa gift exchanges. It randomly assigns participants to each other, ensuring that each person receives a gift from another participant without knowing who their giver is until the exchange occurs.

How it works:

  1. Participants enter their names into the generator.
  2. The generator assigns each participant a Secret Santa recipient.
  3. Participants exchange gifts based on the assignments provided by the generator.
